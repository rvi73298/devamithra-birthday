MUSIC SETUP GUIDE
=================

This folder should contain your background music file.

REQUIRED FILE:
✅ music.mp3 - Soft instrumental background music (20-30 minutes)

🎵 MUSIC RECOMMENDATIONS:

1. **Piano Instrumental**
   - Calm, elegant, perfect for emotional moments
   - Try: "Peaceful Piano" playlists on Spotify/YouTube

2. **Lo-Fi Ambient**
   - Relaxing, modern, perfect for the vibe
   - Try: Lo-Fi Girl, Chillhop Music on YouTube

3. **Soft Orchestral**
   - Classic, beautiful, timeless
   - Try: Film scores, classical compilations

4. **Acoustic Guitar**
   - Warm, intimate, heartfelt
   - Try: Fingerstyle guitar channels

5. **Nature Sounds with Music**
   - Rainfall + piano, Forest + strings
   - Very calming and immersive

📊 MUSIC SPECIFICATIONS:

Format: MP3 (required)
Duration: 20-30 minutes (continuous loop)
Bitrate: 128-192 kbps (recommended)
Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz
File Size: Ideally <50MB

🎧 HOW TO FIND MUSIC:

FREE SOURCES:
- YouTube (Download as MP3 using tools)
- Free Music Archive (freemusicarchive.org)
- Incompetech (incompetech.com)
- Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music)
- Bensound (bensound.com)
- Epidemic Sound (Free tier)

PAID/PREMIUM:
- Spotify (download premium tracks)
- Apple Music
- Soundcloud
- Artlist

🔧 HOW TO PREPARE YOUR MUSIC:

1. Find a 20-30 minute instrumental track
2. Download or record it
3. Convert to MP3 format if needed (use Audacity or online converters)
4. Name it exactly: music.mp3
5. Place it in this folder (assets/)

⚙️ CONVERSION TOOLS (FREE):

Online:
- Online-Convert.com
- CloudConvert.com
- Zamzar.com

Desktop:
- Audacity (audacity-team.org)
- VLC Media Player (convert feature)
- FFmpeg (command line)

🎬 PERFECT SONG STRUCTURE:

0:00 - 2:00     → Soft, welcoming (Loading screen)
2:00 - 8:00     → Warm, emotional (Photos & Letter)
8:00 - 15:00    → Cheerful, celebratory (Cake & celebration)
15:00 - 20:00   → Reflective, peaceful (Memorial & gift)
20:00 - 30:00   → Hopeful, gentle (Final slides & ending)

⏯️ MUSIC LOOP BEHAVIOR:

- Music auto-plays on page load
- Loops continuously throughout presentation
- Volume is normal (user can control via browser)
- No pause between loops

💡 TIPS:

✅ Choose instrumental to avoid lyrics distraction
✅ Keep volume relatively low initially
✅ Ensure smooth looping (no abrupt transitions)
✅ Test on different devices
✅ A good choice sets the perfect mood!

📱 BROWSER AUDIO NOTES:

- Most browsers require user interaction to autoplay
- Click anywhere on page if music doesn't start
- Some browsers may mute autoplay initially
- User can mute/unmute from browser controls

Delete this file after adding your music file.
